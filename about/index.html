alignment

You may find me a little dogmatic and boring as I have a lawful good alignment in d&d system. I am happy with this alignment, although interestingly it is, however, somewhat atypical both in my career and in my industry.

Lawful Good- A lawful good character acts as a good person is expected or required to act. He combines a commitment to oppose evil with the discipline to fight relentlessly. He tells the truth, keeps his word, helps those in need, and speaks out against injustice. A lawful good character hates to see the guilty go unpunished. Lawful good is the best alignment you can be because it combines honor and compassion. However, lawful good can be a dangerous alignment when it restricts freedom and criminalizes self-interest.
